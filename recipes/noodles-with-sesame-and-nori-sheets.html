Noodles With Sesame And Nori Sheets

Description

Super tangy and easy recipe.

Ingredients

Noodles

  • Ramen noodles
  • Salt

Dressing

  • 1 Organic lime, Grated zest and the juice
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 garlic clove, squeezed
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  • 20 gnori sheets, in bite-sized pieces

Steps

Noodles

  1. Cook the noodles in boiling salted water until al dente, drain.

Dressing

  1. Combine the lime zest and juice with the oil, garlic and sesame in a bowl.
  2. Mix the nori into the hot noodles

Tip:

Mix 50 g of baby spinach into the hot noodles.
