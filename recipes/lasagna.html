Not Yo Grandmas Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is by far the best lasagna recipe known to man kind. Tell yo momma, tell yo mail man, that this stuff is off the hook.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spice and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
